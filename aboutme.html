Fuels for my Inner Fire

Used to be a whirlwind on the court, tiny me dodging giants like a futsal firecracker. Handball is my pure adrenaline symphony. But somewhere, the flame flickered, traded for lamplight's glow and my curated tunes. Days now are slow honey, each one a canvas I paint with music and the muffled thump-thump of solo jogs. Work keeps me real, a steady beat in my quiet haven. But the best part? Finding joy in the simple things.Every sunrise paints a brand new picture. Nature's my artist now, every sunrise a masterpiece waiting for my lens. A memory, replaced by my own quiet tune, finding its rhythm in the hush. It's a different kind of thrill, a symphony just for me, played in the peaceful beauty of my own little world.

Obsessions

Black and nude, my soul mates on the color wheel! Black, like a velvet curtain drawn on the stars, a canvas where vibrant hues ignite. Nude, like my own sun-kissed skin, a whisper of warmth that feels like home. Coffee, oh you fragrant enchantress, my morning muse! The aroma, a sunrise symphony in steam, the taste, a velvet hug on my tongue. Brewing beans in the cafe, a chorus of clinking cups and frothy magic, is my haven. And tomyam? A fiesta in a bowl, secrets of chili and lemongrass, a vibrant dance on my taste buds! These are the little loves that make my heart hum, the everyday sparklers that light up my world like fireflies in the night.
